FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

To install Microsoft 365, sign in to your Microsoft account, go to the Microsoft 365 home page, select Install Office, and follow the installation wizard. Make sure your device meets the system requirements.

Go to the Microsoft account sign-in page, click "Forgot my password," enter your email address, and follow the instructions sent to your email or phone to reset your password.

Microsoft 365 includes premium Office apps (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook), 1TB of OneDrive cloud storage, advanced security features, and regular updates. Family plans support up to 6 users.

Sign in to your Microsoft account, go to Services & subscriptions, find your subscription, and select Cancel. You can also manage subscriptions through the Microsoft Store app.

Try restarting the app, checking for updates, running Office repair tool, or signing out and back into your Microsoft account. If issues persist, contact support.

You can upload files through the OneDrive website, use the OneDrive desktop app for automatic syncing, or save directly from Office apps to OneDrive.
